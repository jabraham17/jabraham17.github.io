In the course of some of my work, I have frequently needed a one-stop shop reference for WebAssembly instructions. However, I have been unable to find one that meets all my needs.

My requirements for the information is that it must display the instruction name, the full hex code, the effect the instruction has on the stack, and whether it takes an immediate. I also wanted something that would be updated live (with little to no maintenance) and that included some of the top proposals (ie SIMD and Threading).
